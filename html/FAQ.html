Frequently Asked Questions

How to create a fanfic?

Go to the Create page, fill in all required fields and click the Create button.

What are the size classifications?

Mini: up to 1000 words, Midi: 1000-5000 words, Maxi: over 5000 words.

How to save a fanfic?

Click the "Save" button on any fanfic page to add it to your Saves.

What do age ratings mean?

G: General, PG-13: Teens 13+, R: Restricted, NC-17: Adults 17+, NC-21: Adults 21+

How to change theme?

Go to Settings page and select your preferred theme.
